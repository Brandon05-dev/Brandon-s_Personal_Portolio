Delicious Pilau

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g beef or chicken, cubed
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp pilau masala
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 4 cups beef broth or water
  • Salt to taste
  • Cooking oil

Preparation:

  1. Heat oil in a pot and fry onions until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and stir for a minute.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  4. Add beef/chicken and cook until browned.
  5. Stir in pilau masala and salt.
  6. Add washed rice and mix well.
  7. Pour in beef broth and let it cook until rice is tender.
  8. Fluff the rice and serve hot.

Nutritional Information (Per Serving):

  • Calories: 450 Kcal
  • Protein: 25g
  • Carbohydrates: 60g
  • Fat: 15g
