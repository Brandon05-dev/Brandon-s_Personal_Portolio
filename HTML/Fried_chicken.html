Crispy Fried Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Preparation:

  1. Marinate chicken in buttermilk for at least 2 hours.
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, salt, pepper, paprika, garlic powder, and cayenne.
  3. Coat each chicken piece in the seasoned flour.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan to 175°C (350°F).
  5. Fry chicken until golden brown and crispy (about 12-15 minutes).
  6. Drain on paper towels and serve hot.

Nutritional Information (Per Serving):

  • Calories: 520 kcal
  • Protein: 35g
  • Carbohydrates: 30g
  • Fat: 28g
